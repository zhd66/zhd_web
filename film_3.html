Sin City
Basic Imformation

      Sin city is an action film adapted from Frank Miller's cartoon of the same name and directed by Robert Rodriguez, Frank Miller and Quentin Tarantino. The film tells three stories: Sin City, the big fat kill and that Yellow Bastard. These three stories all take place in a fictional city - the city of sin, in which the bishop, the senator and the police are the root causes of sin. The film was released in the United States on April 1, 2005.
